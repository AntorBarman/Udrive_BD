1. Introduction

Welcome to Udrive Bangladesh ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website udrive.com or use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Book a vehicle
  • List your vehicle as an earner
  • Contact our customer support
  • Participate in promotions or surveys

This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • National ID number
  • Driving license information
  • Payment information
  • Profile photo

2.2 Vehicle Information

If you list your vehicle on our platform, we collect:

  • Vehicle registration details
  • Insurance information
  • Vehicle photos
  • Maintenance records

2.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate our platform:

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • Location information (with your consent)
  • Booking history and preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for various purposes, including:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To verify your identity and driving credentials
  • To communicate about your bookings
  • To provide customer support

3.2 Platform Improvement

  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To conduct research and analysis
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3.3 Communication

  • To send important updates and notifications
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • To gather feedback about our services

3.4 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To enforce our terms and conditions
  • To protect our rights and property
  • To resolve disputes

4. Information Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 With Other Users

When you book a vehicle, we share necessary information with the vehicle owner:

  • Your name and profile photo
  • Contact information (only for booking-related communication)
  • Booking details (dates, location, etc.)

When you list a vehicle, we share with renters:

  • Your first name and profile photo
  • Vehicle information and photos
  • Your average rating (if applicable)

4.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Payment processing
  • Background verification
  • Customer support
  • Marketing and analytics
  • Cloud storage and hosting

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid requests by public authorities
  • Court orders or legal processes
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • To investigate potential violations

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5.1 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Regular security audits and testing
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Data encryption at rest

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Typically, we retain:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • Booking records: 7 years for tax purposes
  • Communication data: 2 years
  • Payment information: As required by law

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Obtain a copy of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities

6.2 Marketing Preferences

You can control your marketing preferences:

  • Opt-out of promotional emails
  • Adjust push notification settings
  • Manage cookie preferences

6.3 Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by:

  • Using the delete account feature in settings
  • Contacting our support team

Note: Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

7. Cookies & Tracking

7.1 Types of Cookies

We use various types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for platform functionality
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand user behavior
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver relevant ads

7.2 Third-Party Tracking

We may use third-party services that use tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Facebook Pixel for advertising
  • Payment processors for transaction security

7.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

If you believe we might have any information from or about a child under 18, please contact us immediately.

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
